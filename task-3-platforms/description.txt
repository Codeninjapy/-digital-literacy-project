Task 3 – Platforms

This task involves exploring both coding and collaboration platforms to understand their role in learning and academic development. For the coding part, I created an account on HackerRank and
successfully completed a beginner-level challenge, which helped me understand how such platforms are used for practicing programming skills and improving problem-solving abilities.

For the collaboration part, I created a Google Form titled “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz” consisting of five questions related to digital literacy. The form included both multiple-choice
and short answer questions. I also linked the form to a response sheet to collect and analyze responses. This activity helped me understand how online tools can be used for creating quizzes, 
collecting data, and collaborating effectively.
