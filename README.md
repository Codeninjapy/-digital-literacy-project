Digital Literacy Project
📌 Project Overview

The Digital Literacy Project aims to spread awareness about the safe, effective, and responsible use of digital technologies. It focuses on helping individuals understand basic digital tools, online safety, communication etiquette, and cyber awareness.

Digital literacy is essential in today’s world as it enables people to participate confidently in the digital environment and avoid risks like scams, misinformation, and cyber threats.

🎯 Objectives
To educate users about basic digital tools and platforms
To promote safe internet practices
To create awareness about cyber security and online fraud
To improve communication skills through digital platforms
To encourage responsible use of social media
