🛡️ Prevention Checklist – Cybercrime Awareness

✅ Do’s (Safety Measures)
Always verify the source before clicking on any links or responding to messages.
Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Be cautious while sharing personal or financial information online.
Always check the URL of websites before entering sensitive details.
Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
Keep your devices and applications updated regularly.
Regularly monitor your bank transactions and account activity.

💳 UPI / Online Payment Safety
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
Do not scan QR codes or click on links to receive money.

🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
Report cybercrime incidents on: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
Call the cybercrime helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance
